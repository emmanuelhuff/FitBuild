Welcome to FitBuild! This is your first step towards getting fit.

Often times when people first begin their fitness journeys, they become confused on what exactly they should do or how to organize their own routines. FitBuild is a webapp that alleviates that confusion by providing you the means to build a fitness routine custom for you.

Whether your goal is to work your way up to running a marathon, gaining muscle, or even simply working off those extra thanksgiving pounds with some cardio, we are here to help!
