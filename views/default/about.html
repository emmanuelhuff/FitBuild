About Me

Hello! My name is Emmanuel Huff. I am a fourth year at the University of California: Santa Cruz. I currently study Computer Science and hope to complete my Bachelor of Science by this school year. While I am from San Diego, California, I spent a semester at the Universiteit Utrecht where I took a software testing course while simultaneously created a text-based rpg game based on the retro game titled "Rogue". In the future I wish to be working on similar projects that really seek to intrigue and keep me busy. In addition, I am hoping to work somewhere in the Bay Area this summer, provided an offer is available to me. I also enjoy playing Soccer, Volleyball, and Basketball.

I created FitBuild because I too had a struggle with getting into fitness. Who wouldn't? The idea of working out at a gym was very scary at first, especially when I had no one to teach me what routines I really should be doing. With FitBuild I hope that I can help alleviate your stress for such situations.
